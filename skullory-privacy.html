Privacy Policy

App: Skullory
Developer / Data Controller: Davide Ghelfi
Contact: dade.ghelfi@gmail.com
Effective date: 15 September 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Skullory handles information. The app does not create user accounts and does not ask you to provide personal information.

1) What the app does and doesn’t do

  • No analytics and no cookies. The game does not use third-party analytics SDKs and does not place tracking cookies.
  • Game data stays on your device. Progress, settings, and the “premium unlocked” state are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to the developer.
  • In-app purchases (IAP). Payments are processed by Google Play Billing. The developer does not receive or store your full payment details. A non-identifying flag (e.g., premium/unlocked) may be stored locally to enable features.
  • Advertising. This app uses Google AdMob to serve ads. This app displays ads through Google AdMob and may use your device’s Advertising ID. AdMob may collect certain information automatically to show, measure, and prevent abuse of ads (see Section 2).

2) Data that may be collected via third-party SDKs (Google AdMob)

  • Advertising ID (a resettable device identifier) and related device information.
  • Approximate location (e.g., country/region inferred from IP address).
  • App interactions with ads (such as ad views, clicks, or impressions).
  • Diagnostics and performance data (e.g., crash/error information, load failures) for reliability and fraud prevention.

Purposes: advertising & marketing, measurement/analytics, and fraud prevention/security.
Encryption: data transmitted by the Mobile Ads SDK is encrypted in transit.
Sharing: these data may be shared with Google (AdMob) and its measurement partners as described in Google’s policies.

Links to Google policies:

3) Consent and your choices

If you are in a region where consent is required (e.g., EEA/UK), the app shows a User Messaging Platform (UMP) prompt. You can:

  • Give or refuse consent to personalized ads. If you refuse, you may still see non-personalized ads.
  • Change your choice later by reopening the consent prompt from the app’s Privacy screen (if provided) or by reinstalling the app to trigger the prompt again.

You can also control ad personalization at the system level by resetting/removing your device’s Advertising ID and/or opting out of ad personalization in Android settings.

4) Data we do not collect

We do not collect: your name, email, phone number, contacts, precise (GPS) location, photos, microphone/audio, or any content you create. The app does not contain social login and does not allow account creation.

5) Children’s privacy

Skullory is not directed to children and should be used by individuals who are at least the age required by local law to give valid consent (e.g., 16 in the EEA, 13 in the U.S.). If you believe a child has provided personal data via the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

6) Data retention

  • On-device game data (progress/settings/premium) remain on your device until you delete the app or clear the app’s data.
  • Ad-related data are retained by Google according to Google’s policies; the developer does not control these retention periods.

7) Data sharing and international transfers

The developer does not sell your data. Data used for ads may be shared with Google (AdMob) and its partners. Because Google operates globally, such data may be processed on servers outside your country; Google uses appropriate safeguards as described in its policies.

8) Security

The app uses secure communication (encryption in transit) for SDK traffic. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. Use the app at your own discretion.

9) Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights such as access, correction, objection, withdrawal of consent, and deletion. For data processed by Google (AdMob), please refer to Google’s privacy tools and settings. For any questions to the developer, contact dade.ghelfi@gmail.com.

10) Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the Effective date above. Continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the updated Policy.
