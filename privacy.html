Privacy Policy – Skullory

Last updated: 03 Sep 2025

Skullory is a free game by DadeGames. The app shows ads using Google AdMob and asks for consent in the EEA/UK using Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP).

Data used

  • Advertising identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID)
  • Approximate location (for ad serving)
  • Crash/performance data

How we use data

  • To show ads with Google AdMob.
  • In the EEA/UK we use Google UMP to ask for consent. Based on your choice we show personalised or non-personalised ads.

Your choices

  • You can change or reset your choice by clearing the app’s data or reinstalling the app.
  • You can reset the Android Advertising ID from your device settings.

Third-party

Google is an independent controller for the data it processes. See Google Privacy Policy and How Google uses information for ads.
